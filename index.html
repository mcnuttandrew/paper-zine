How to read an academic paper

a non definitive guide

By Andrew McNutt
Printable version Online version

In this zine I provide an overview of my approach to reading an academic paper. For more details please read the zine! If printing the zine, it is best constructed using a printer that can print on both sides of a piece of paper. Ensure that you select "flip on short edge" configuration. It can be printed in black and white with no loss of quality!
